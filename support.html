Common Queries

Why are my plant leaves turning yellow?

Yellowing leaves can be due to overwatering, underwatering, or poor lighting. Make sure the plant gets enough indirect light and the soil drains well.

How often should I water my indoor plants?

Most indoor plants prefer watering once a week. Check if the top inch of the soil is dry before watering.

Which plants are best for purifying air?

Snake plant, Spider plant, and Areca palm are great options for air purification.

What is the best fertilizer for trees?

Use a balanced NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium) fertilizer in the ratio 10:10:10 or 14:14:14 during the growing season.
