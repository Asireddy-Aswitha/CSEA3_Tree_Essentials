Tree Essentials Help Center

Order Details

You can track your order status by visiting the "Track Order" option on our homepage. You’ll need your Order ID and registered email address.

If your product hasn’t shipped yet, you can request a cancellation or make changes by contacting our support team via live chat or email.

Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking link with real-time updates from our logistics partners.

Product Details

We offer a wide variety of indoor, outdoor, flowering, and air-purifying plants. Each product listing includes the plant type, care instructions, and included pots or accessories.

If you're unsure about what plant suits your space, our expert support is available 24/7 to guide you in choosing the right products.

All plants are shipped in secure eco-friendly packaging with a detailed care guide.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How can I change my shipping address?
Please contact our support team within 12 hours of placing the order to update your shipping address.
What should I do if my plant arrives damaged?
We’re sorry! Please share a photo of the plant within 24 hours of delivery and we’ll send a free replacement.
Are pots included with the plant?
Yes, unless stated otherwise. Each product page clearly mentions what's included.
Can I return my plant?
Plants are perishable, so we do not accept returns. However, we offer refunds or replacements for damaged items.
